Welcome

This site summarizes my essay on how web browsers began, how they competed during the Browser Wars, how mobile changed everything, why security matters, and where browsers may be headed next with AI.

Web browsers are essential tools that connect us to work, school, communication, entertainment, and information across the the internet. they act as gateways that translate data from web servers into text, images,videos,and interactive content we use every day.
